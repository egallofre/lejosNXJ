To uninstall on Safari:
  1. Click the Safari icon at the top of your computer > Preferences > Extensions. Uninstall the most recent installed applications.
  2. Additionally, try the Applications & Library folders of your hard drive. Look for extensions and/or plugins within these folders and move to your trash.
